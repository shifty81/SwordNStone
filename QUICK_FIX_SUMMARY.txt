GUI RENDERING FIX - QUICK SUMMARY
==================================

PROBLEM: GUI elements looked "jaggedly assembled" and distorted

ROOT CAUSE: Textures being stretched too much
- Unit frames: 256x128 pixels (2x stretch of 128x96 PNG)
- Action slots: 64x64 pixels (1.33x stretch of 48x48 PNG)

SOLUTION: Reduce stretching to match natural asset sizes

CHANGES MADE:
=============

1. GuiFrameRenderer.ci.cs
   - Added panel_dark.png background before frame borders
   - Creates proper layered appearance

2. GuiWoWUnitFrames.ci.cs  
   - frameWidth: 256 → 192  (25% smaller)
   - frameHeight: 128 → 112 (12% smaller)
   - Result: Less pixelation, cleaner borders

3. GuiWoWActionBars.ci.cs
   - ButtonSize: 64px → 48px (matches PNG size)
   - ButtonSpacing: 10px → 8px (maintains layout)
   - Result: Crisp slots, no stretching

EXPECTED IMPROVEMENTS:
======================
✅ Unit frames: Cleaner, less pixelated
✅ Action bar: Crisp slots at natural size
✅ Frames: Proper dark backgrounds
✅ Overall: Professional, polished appearance
